Easy Build Mod is a mod that makes it easier to place or destroy blocks, thus improving the efficiency of construction.
There are three new items:
1. Item Pickup Magnet
    When turned on, it can increase the range of item pickup.
2. Block Placement Assistant
    It can quickly fill the rectangular area with blocks.
3. Block Destruction Assistant
    It can quickly destroy the blocks or walls in the rectangular area.
All three items can be crafted with 20 iron ingots or lead ingots.

便捷建造模组是一个可以让你更容易放置和破坏方块，从而提高建造效率的模组。
一共有三种新增物品：
1. 物品拾取磁铁
    开启后可以增大物品拾取范围。
2. 物块放置助手
    可以将矩形区域快速用方块填充。
3. 物块破坏助手
    可以快速破坏矩形区域内的方块或墙壁。
以上三种物品都只需要20个铁锭或铅锭即可合成。
